FAQ

Questions, answered.

How fast will my website be live?

expand_more

Your website goes live within 24–48 hours of receiving your business info, photos, and branding. You review a live preview before anything goes public.

What counts as a monthly edit?

expand_more

An edit is one change request — swap photos, update copy or pricing, add a promo banner, change your hours, or refresh a section. Send your requests anytime and we ship them, usually within 48 hours. Unused edits don't roll over.

What does the $49/mo cover?

expand_more

Fast global hosting, SSL certificate, daily backups, uptime monitoring, your included monthly edits — plus full access to Velosify CRM for lead generation and pipeline management. It's a separate subscription that starts when your site goes live, and you can cancel anytime.

Do I own my website?

expand_more

Yes. The one-time build fee buys your design outright. Your domain stays in your name, and if you ever leave, we hand over your site files — no hostage situations.

Can I upgrade my package later?

expand_more

Anytime. Pay the difference between packages and we'll expand your site — add pages, bump your monthly edit count, or both. Your hosting stays at the same $49/mo.

What kind of support do you offer?

expand_more

Submit a support ticket right inside Velosify CRM — most tickets get a reply within a few hours during business days. Edit requests go through the same place. If your site ever goes down, fixing it is on us, not your edit count.
