FAQ

Questions, answered.

How is this different from other CRMs?

expand_more

Other CRMs assume you already have leads. Velosify is a tracking tool PLUS a lead generator that scrapes high-intent local data automatically.

Are there any usage fees inside the app?

expand_more

Your $49/month plan includes 18,000 lead scrapes and 3,000 phone-number identifications each month — both allowances reset on your billing date. If you go over either limit, you pay a small per-use fee (just a fraction of a penny per number) until the next reset. For most users the included quota is plenty to stay busy all month.

Can I cancel anytime?

expand_more

Yes. No contracts, no commitments. Cancel from Settings → Billing in one click and you won't be charged again. You keep access until the end of your current billing period.

Do I need to install anything?

expand_more

No. It's 100% web-based and responsive. Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile with zero downloads required.

Is my data secure?

expand_more

Every user gets a fully isolated workspace. Your data is encrypted and stored with daily automated backups.

What kind of support do you offer?

expand_more

Reach our support team at support@velosify.ai — most messages get a reply within a few hours during business days. Onboarding help, CSV import, and custom-field setup are included with every plan at no extra cost.
